502 — Per-Phase TP/SL Methods

TL;DR — Two SL families. OTE.entry uses commitment_extreme_level — a Shadow Thesis snap of lower_structure.range_low/high at the watch-phase open; owner is hypothesis.py, not EC. EX.entry uses watch_range_extreme — a Shadow Thesis running extreme tracked bar-by-bar from the watch period open (range_high for short trade / range_low for long trade). Global SL band 15–25 pips (EURUSD): floor widens to 15, cap above 25 rejects without spending budget. TP: Phase D targets pd_midpoint (50% HTF PD range). Phase B and A target the 90% objective-progress level capped at 2.5R. Phase C is pending.
→ 501 Entry Details (phases, budgets, triggers)

00 SL Method Overview

Each active entry path reads its SL anchor from a Shadow Thesis field in debug_facts. There are two families: a one-time snap (commitment_extreme_level) used by OTE.entry, and a running extremum (watch_range_extreme) used by EX.entry. 

Phase          Path              Entry type   SL field                              Shadow owner
────────────   ───────────────   ──────────   ───────────────────────────────────   ─────────────
D.watch        D.watch_pathA     EX           phase_d_shadow_watch_range_extreme    PhaseDShadow
B.watch        B.watch_pathA/B   OTE          phase_b_shadow_commitment_extreme_level PhaseBShadow
A.watch        A.watch_pathA     OTE          phase_a_shadow_commitment_extreme_level PhaseAShadow
A.watch_wk     A.watch_weaken_ex EX           phase_a_shadow_watch_range_extreme    PhaseAShadow
C.pullback     C.pullback_pathA  OTE          phase_c_shadow_commitment_extreme_level (pending)
C.pullback_wk  C.pullback_wk_ex  EX           — (pending)                           —
All SL anchors are Shadow Thesis fields emitted into debug_facts. Layer 5 reads a scalar — it does not scan events or compute extremes itself.

01 OTE SL — commitment_extreme_level (Shadow Thesis snap)

commitment_extreme_level is a snapshot of lower_structure.range_low (long trade) or lower_structure.range_high (short trade) taken at watch-phase open. It is a Shadow Thesis field maintained by hypothesis.py — not an EC scan of internal structure events. 

source:
  lower_structure.range_low  (long trade  — SL below entry)
  lower_structure.range_high (short trade — SL above entry)

PhaseBShadow:
  snap point: C→B transition bar (single snap, never updated during B.watch hold)
  debug_facts key: phase_b_shadow_commitment_extreme_level

PhaseAShadow:
  snap point: every A.watch open:
    1. initial B→A transition
    2. A.watch_weaken → A.watch recovery
    3. advance gate re-arm (after pro_extreme_at_weaken advances past last OTE level)
  debug_facts key: phase_a_shadow_commitment_extreme_level
  note: LTF ratchet ensures range_low deepens / range_high rises on each episode open

PhaseCshadow (pending):
  snap point: C.pullback entry bar
  debug_facts key: phase_c_shadow_commitment_extreme_level
This is the same snap mechanism for all three phases — read lower_structure at the phase-open event and lock the value. No window scan, no EC function call, no internal event iteration.
Why not an EC scan design note

An earlier design computed commitment_extreme_level by scanning internal_structure_sequence events between two timestamps (counter iChoCh → pro iChoCh) looking for the deepest levelPrice. That approach was abandoned because:

  • The snap-at-open mechanism is simpler and already used for PhaseBShadow — no new EC function needed.
  • lower_structure.range_low/high is a wick-level bar extreme, which gives a more conservative (wider) anchor than a close-through levelPrice.
  • LTF ratchet guarantees the snap deepens on each episode, so re-snapping at every A.watch open is always at least as conservative as a single B→A snap.

02 EX SL — watch_range_extreme (Shadow Thesis running extreme)

watch_range_extreme is a running extremum tracked bar-by-bar during the watch period. Updated by hypothesis.py on each hold bar; stops tracking when the phase exits. Identical mechanism for Phase D and Phase A.watch_weaken. 

PhaseDShadow.watch_range_extreme:
  tracked from: D.watch open
  tracked until: D→C transition
  long HTF (short trade):  running max of lower_structure.range_high (SL above entry)
  short HTF (long trade):  running min of lower_structure.range_low  (SL below entry)
  debug_facts key: phase_d_shadow_watch_range_extreme

PhaseAShadow.watch_range_extreme:
  tracked from: A.watch_weaken open
  tracked until: exit from A.watch_weaken (→ C.pullback or → A.watch recovery)
  long HTF (long trade):   running min of lower_structure.range_low  (SL below entry)
  short HTF (short trade): running max of lower_structure.range_high (SL above entry)
  debug_facts key: phase_a_shadow_watch_range_extreme

PhaseCshadow EX (pending):
  tracked from: C.pullback_weaken open
  source anchor: D.watch range_extreme tentatively — pending confirmation
watch_range_extreme for EX.entry is not clamped against commitment_extreme_level. The two anchors are independent. EX.entry uses only watch_range_extreme.

03 SL Application

Once the anchor is known, the SL price is anchor ± buffer.

$$ SL_{\text{long}} = \text{anchor} - \text{sl\_buffer\_pips} \times \text{pip\_size} \qquad SL_{\text{short}} = \text{anchor} + \text{sl\_buffer\_pips} \times \text{pip\_size} $$ 
sl_pips = |entry_price - sl_raw| / pip_size

After computing sl_raw, the global SL band rules below apply.

04 Global SL Band Rules (EURUSD)

These rules apply to every entry slot regardless of phase or entry type. The band is a property of the instrument, configured in config.yaml asset_geometry.

Rule 1 — Floor: sl_pips < 15 → widen to minimum non-stale, no budget spend

If the computed SL is closer than 15 pips to entry, automatically widen it outward to exactly 15 pips. The trade proceeds with the floored SL. Does not consume budget on its own — budget is consumed only if the resulting EntryIntent fires.

sl_price = entry ∓ min_sl_pips × pip_size   (long: minus, short: plus)
Rule 2 — Cap: sl_pips > 25 → reject, do not wind down SL_too_wide, no budget spend

If the computed SL exceeds 25 pips, the trade idea is rejected. The SL is not wound down to 25 — the anchor is what it is and the setup is geometrically invalid at this moment.

return SkipIntent(reason="SL_too_wide", stale=False)
# budget NOT consumed — geometry failure ≠ trade placed

For EX slots, the dedup guard (_a_watch_ex_fired) seals the EX slot after the first stale rejection regardless of budget state. For OTE slots, the engine rechecks geometry on the next valid trigger sequence.

Rule 3 — runway_too_short does not consume budget non-stale retry

A runway_too_short (rr < 1.75) rejection is non-stale. The trigger event may retry while within max_trigger_age_bars. Budget is only consumed when an EntryIntent actually fires.

Budget is consumed by trades placed, not by geometry checks. Floor (Rule 1) widens and enters. Cap (Rule 2) rejects without spending budget. Both are non-stale — the DAG and dedup guards own phase termination, not the SL band.

05 Per-Phase TP/SL Reference

TP formulas and SL shadow fields for each active path. Phase C is pending implementation; its rows are included for completeness.

Phase D

D.watch_pathA (EX.entry):
  trade_direction = opposite of direction  ← counter-HTF (short in bullish HTF)
  SL = phase_d_shadow_watch_range_extreme ± sl_buffer_pips
       ← running max of lower_structure.range_high during D.watch hold (short trade)
       ← running min of lower_structure.range_low during D.watch hold (long trade)
       !! floor: sl_pips < 15 → widen outward to 15 pips
       !! cap:   sl_pips > 25 → SL_too_wide (stale=False); no budget consumed; trigger re-evaluates until age-out
  TP  = higher_structure.pd_midpoint   ← 50% of active HTF PD range
  RR gate: rr < 1.75 → runway_too_short (retryable); rr ≥ 1.75 → take
  evidence_kind = "watch_extreme"

Phase B

B.watch_pathA / B.watch_pathB (OTE.entry):
  trade_direction = direction  ← pro-HTF (long in bullish HTF)
  SL = phase_b_shadow_commitment_extreme_level ± sl_buffer_pips
       ← lower_structure.range_low  snapped at C→B bar (long trade)
       ← lower_structure.range_high snapped at C→B bar (short trade)
       ← locked for the entire B.watch episode; never updated during hold
       !! floor: sl_pips < 15 → widen outward
       !! cap:   sl_pips > 25 → SL_too_wide (stale=False); episode not consumed; trigger re-evaluates until age-out
  TP  = min(progress_pips, 2.5 × sl_pips) toward 90% HTF objective level
        progress_level = pd_low  + 0.90 × span  (long)
                       = pd_high − 0.90 × span  (short)
        tp_pips = min(|progress_level − entry|, 2.5 × sl_pips) / pip_size
  RR gate: rr < 1.75 → runway_too_short (retryable); rr ≥ 1.75 → take
  evidence_kind = "b_watch_commitment"
  htf_sd_zone_tapped: analysis tag only — not an entry gate

Phase A

A.watch_pathA (OTE.entry):
  trade_direction = direction  ← pro-HTF
  SL = phase_a_shadow_commitment_extreme_level ± sl_buffer_pips
       ← lower_structure.range_low  snapped at each A.watch open (long trade)
       ← lower_structure.range_high snapped at each A.watch open (short trade)
       ← re-snapped: B→A, weaken→watch recovery, advance gate re-arm
       !! floor: sl_pips < 15 → widen outward
       !! cap:   sl_pips > 25 → SL_too_wide (stale=False); does not block episode; new sequence re-arms
  TP  = same formula as Phase B (90% objective, capped at 2.5R)
  RR gate: same 1.75 floor
  evidence_kind = "a_watch_commitment"
  re-arm gate (weaken-recovery path only):
    OTE re-arms only when pro_extreme_at_weaken advances past _a_watch_last_ote_pro_extreme

A.watch_weaken_ex (EX.entry):
  trade_direction = direction  ← pro-HTF
  SL = phase_a_shadow_watch_range_extreme ± sl_buffer_pips
       ← running min of lower_structure.range_low  from A.watch_weaken open (long trade)
       ← running max of lower_structure.range_high from A.watch_weaken open (short trade)
       ← stops tracking on exit from A.watch_weaken
       ← no clamp against commitment_extreme_level
       !! floor / cap: same 15–25 pip band
  TP  = same formula as Phase B
  RR gate: same 1.75 floor
  evidence_kind = "a_watch_ex"
  post-trade: Layer 5 treats Phase A as exhausted (budget forced to 3); no further A entries fire.
             Immediate DAG X.thesis_over is deferred — DAG transitions via phase_a_thesis_matured
             (price progress). See V1 note in docs/501-entry-details.html.

06 Issues

Completed · 2026-06-30

PhaseAShadow.commitment_extreme_level added. hypothesis.py snaps the A-owned field at each A.watch open (B→A and A.watch_weaken → A.watch recovery). A.watch_pathA reads this Phase A snap, not the B-owned transition field.

Completed · 2026-06-30

PhaseAShadow.watch_range_extreme tracking added. hypothesis.py tracks the running A.watch_weaken SL anchor bar-by-bar: long tracks minimum range_low; short tracks maximum range_high.

Pending

Phase C SL/TP formulas. C.pullback_pathA TP (SD zone vs fixed fallback) and C.pullback_weaken_ex SL source (which watch_range_extreme) are pending confirmation. Phase C implementation is deferred until Phase A is verified.

Out of Scope

Wick-level vs close-through anchor. lower_structure.range_low/high is a wick-level bar extreme. The earlier EC scan approach used levelPrice (close-confirmed structural break price). Current design uses wick-level — it gives a wider, more conservative anchor. Re-evaluating anchor tightness is deferred to post null-case backtest.

Deferred

Daily ATR shape target. A future Layer 6 extension may replace fixed-formula TP with a Daily ATR fraction target per asset. Current: enabled: false in config. Pending post-backtest review.

Archived

EC scan approach removed. An earlier design computed commitment_extreme by scanning internal_structure_sequence events between two timestamps using a _commitment_extreme_level() helper in evidence_compiler.py. That function and its caller were removed. Shadow Thesis snap is the canonical mechanism.
